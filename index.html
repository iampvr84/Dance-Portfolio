The artist's journey

Grace born from devotion and discipline

Vanshikasri Palanivel Rajan began her Bharathanatyam journey at a young age, drawn by the rhythmic language of this ancient classical art form. Rooted in the temple traditions of Tamil Nadu, Bharathanatyam weaves together nritta (pure dance), nritya (expressive dance), and natya (drama) into a seamless offering to the divine.

Under the guidance of her guru, Vanshikasri has developed a practice that honours the classical form while channelling genuine emotion and devotion. Every adavu, every mudra, every glance of the eye is an expression of years of patient training and deep love for the art.

Her performances span sabhas, cultural festivals, and temple stages — each one a conversation between the dancer, the music, and the divine presence she invites into the space.
